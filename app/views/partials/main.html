About Us

"I Am The Answer" is a famous dialogue from the Malayalam cult comedy movie Mazha Peyyunnu Maddhalam Kottunnu directed by Priyadarshan. There will be only very few Malayalees (people from the state of Kerala in India) who would not have seen that movie.

The intention of this project is to be a database of Malayalam Movie Dialogues like an IMDb is for Movies.
